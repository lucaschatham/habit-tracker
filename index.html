A public experiment in accountability. I track 24 daily habits in Streaks on my iPhone. Every night at midnight, a script pulls the raw data from Streaks via iCloud, updates this page, and pushes it live. No manual edits, no filtering. Data may lag up to 24 hours. Green = hit target. Yellow = close, but either fell short or overshot the goal. Red = missed. Experiment started March 12, 2026 (red line).
